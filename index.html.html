SYNOPSIS

The Legend of 1900 tells The story of an orphan who was found on a ship on The first day of The 20th century He had a talent for music and learned to play the piano by himself. Later, he survived on the ship by playing the piano and was praised for dancing with the waves He never got off the boat in his whole life, when he met the girl who fell in love with him at first sight, when he had the chance to release a record and let more people meet his wonderful music, he hesitated, either because of the girl he liked, or because of another chance in life to get off the boat. But he still could not give up his sea, his life.
